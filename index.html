Our Programs

Four Steps Toward a Bright Future

Each grade is carefully designed around the developmental milestones of that age group.

👶

Playgroup

First steps into social learning, making friends, building trust, and exploring the world through sensory play.

Age: 1.5 – 2.5 yrs
🌱

Nursery

Language, listening, and creativity bloom here, a world of stories, songs, and simple problem-solving.

Age: 2.5 – 3.5 yrs
🎨

Junior KG

Pre-reading, counting, and artistic expression come alive in a structured yet joyful learning environment.

Age: 3.5 – 4.5 yrs
🚀

Senior KG

School readiness, critical thinking, and leadership confidence and preparing children for their next big chapter.

Age: 4.5 – 5.5 yrs
Why Choose Us

Due To The Little Difference We Make In Children's Lives

Parents across two branches trust us for one simple reason — we treat every child like our OWN.

🌍

Regional Cultural Connect

Children celebrate local festivals, learn folk songs, and are introduced to Marathi culture alongside English, keeping roots alive from day one.

🧑‍🏫

Trained Teaching Staff

Our educators hold certified qualifications and bring warmth, patience, and structured care to every classroom.

🎵

Music & Movement

Daily rhythm sessions, dance, and instrument exploration keep young bodies and minds energized.

🌿

Nature & Garden Time

Our outdoor green corner teaches children about plants, seasons, and caring for the earth.

🤝

Active Parent Involvement

Monthly parent-teacher meetups, workshops and open classroom days keep families engaged.

🏅

NEP-Based Curriculum

Our curriculum aligns with the National Education Policy 2020 — play-based, activity-driven, and focused on holistic development.
